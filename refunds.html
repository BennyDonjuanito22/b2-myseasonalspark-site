Refund & Return Policy

Effective Date: February 11, 2026

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

My Seasonal Spark is a trade name operated by ASI Innovative Solutions LLC. All refunds are processed by ASI Innovative Solutions LLC.

1. Return Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. Most items can be returned within 30 days of delivery.

2. Return Eligibility

2.1 Eligible for Return

  • Items in original, unused condition
  • Items in original packaging with tags attached
  • Items returned within 30 days of delivery

2.2 Not Eligible for Return

  • Items marked as "Final Sale" or "Non-Returnable"
  • Items that have been used, worn, or damaged by customer
  • Items without original packaging
  • Personalized or custom items
  • Clearance items (unless defective)
  • Gift cards

3. How to Return

  1. Contact us: Email support@myseasonalspark.com with your order number
  2. Receive authorization: We'll send return instructions and a return label (if applicable)
  3. Pack securely: Use original packaging if possible
  4. Ship: Send the item back using the provided instructions
  5. Refund: Processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the return

4. Return Shipping

Reason Who Pays Shipping
Defective or damaged item We provide prepaid label
Wrong item shipped We provide prepaid label
Changed mind / didn't like Customer pays return shipping
Ordered wrong size/style Customer pays return shipping

5. Refunds

5.1 Refund Amount

  • Full refund: Item defective, wrong item shipped
  • Product price only: Changed mind returns (original shipping not refunded)
  • Restocking fee: May apply to some items (clearly noted)

5.2 Refund Processing

  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the return
  • Refunded to original payment method
  • May take additional 5-10 days to appear in your account

6. Damaged or Defective Items

If your item arrives damaged or defective:

  1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  2. Include photos of the damage
  3. We'll arrange for a replacement or full refund

7. Exchanges

We don't offer direct exchanges. To get a different item:

  1. Return the original item for a refund
  2. Place a new order for the desired item

8. Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled before shipping
  • Contact us immediately if you need to cancel
  • Once shipped, you must follow the return process

9. Lost Packages

If your package shows delivered but you didn't receive it:

  1. Check with neighbors and around your property
  2. Contact us within 7 days of delivery date
  3. We'll investigate with the carrier
  4. If confirmed lost, we'll send a replacement or refund
