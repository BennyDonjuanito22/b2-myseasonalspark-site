Shipping Policy

Effective Date: February 11, 2026

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

My Seasonal Spark is a trade name operated by ASI Innovative Solutions LLC.

1. Processing Time

  • Standard orders: 1-3 business days
  • Pre-orders: Ships on specified date
  • Custom/personalized: 3-5 business days

Processing time does not include shipping transit time.

2. Shipping Methods & Times

Method Estimated Transit Cost
Standard Shipping 5-7 business days Calculated at checkout
Expedited Shipping 2-3 business days Calculated at checkout
Express/Overnight 1-2 business days Calculated at checkout

Free shipping may be available on orders over a certain amount (displayed at checkout).

3. Shipping Locations

3.1 United States

We ship to all 50 states including Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories.

  • Alaska, Hawaii, and territories may have longer transit times
  • Some oversized items may have shipping restrictions

3.2 International

Currently, we ship within the United States only. International shipping may be available in the future.

4. Shipping Carriers

We use trusted carriers including:

  • USPS (United States Postal Service)
  • UPS (United Parcel Service)
  • FedEx

5. Order Tracking

  • Tracking number sent via email when order ships
  • Track your order on the carrier's website
  • Contact us if tracking shows no updates after 5 days

6. Delivery

6.1 Delivery Confirmation

  • Most packages are delivered without signature
  • Signature confirmation available for high-value orders
  • Delivered to address provided at checkout

6.2 Delivery Issues

If your package shows delivered but you don't have it:

  1. Check around your property and with neighbors
  2. Allow 24-48 hours (packages sometimes scan early)
  3. Contact us if still not received

7. Shipping Address

  • Ensure your shipping address is correct at checkout
  • We are not responsible for packages delivered to incorrect addresses provided by customers
  • Address changes may be possible before shipping - contact us immediately

8. P.O. Boxes & APO/FPO

  • We ship to P.O. Boxes via USPS
  • APO/FPO military addresses accepted
  • Some oversized items cannot ship to P.O. Boxes

9. Shipping Delays

Delays may occur due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Carrier delays
  • High-volume periods (holidays)
  • Incorrect address information

We are not responsible for carrier delays but will assist with tracking issues.

10. Lost or Damaged Packages

Damaged in transit: Contact us within 48 hours with photos. We'll arrange replacement or refund.

Lost packages: Contact us after 7 days past expected delivery. We'll investigate and resolve.

11. Holiday Shipping

During peak seasons:

  • Order early to ensure delivery by holidays
  • Processing times may be extended
  • Carrier transit times may be longer
  • Check our website for holiday shipping deadlines
